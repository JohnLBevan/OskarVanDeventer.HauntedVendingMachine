Oskar van Deventer's Haunted Vending Machine Puzzle

Your browser does not support HTML5 Canvas, required for this game. Please see this Wiki article for details of browsers supporting this feature.

How to Play

  • Use the buttons or your keyboard's number keys to drop coins in the numberred slots.
  • When a coin hits the top of a switch it sticks.
  • When a coin hits the bottom of a switch it goes through and toggles the switch.
  • When a coin hits a coin it rolls to the other side of the switch.
